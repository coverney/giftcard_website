We want to help!

We are engineering students who want to contribute our small forces in the face of the coronavirus pandemic. This idea of building a website that promotes giftcard selling for the local businesses quickly comes into fruition. We want to help local restaurants and shops survive through the pandemic and we hope you can lend us a hand too!
